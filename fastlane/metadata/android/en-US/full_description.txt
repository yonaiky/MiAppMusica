<p>
  Fork of <a href="https://github.com/fast4x/RiMusic">RiMusic</a>, performance is our top priority.
</p>

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
  <li>Support multiple languages</li>
  <li>Friendly designs</li>
  <li>Cache or download songs for offline playback</li>
  <li>Background playback</li>
  <li>Statistics and reports (stored and processed locally)</li>
  <li>Various audio visualizers (some require mic permission, but disabled by default)</li>
  <li>Sleep timer</li>
  <li>Audio control: playback speed, pitch, normalization, skip silence</li>
  <li>Support for Android Auto, Android TV, and YouTube video</li>
  <li>Update notification</li>
  <li>Export cached/downloaded songs</li>
  <li>Save database and settings</li>
  <li>Play music even when not connected to the internet</li>
  <li>And many more...</li>
</ul>
